Mexico

Mexico City is the capital and largest city of the United Mexican States (Mexico), located in the country's central high-altitude Valley of Mexico. The town began with the Aztecs, and it was called Tenochtitlan. During the past century, it has become a vibrant modern city, including the historic Centro Histórico and Chapultepec, a big park with many museums, the zoo, libraries, and an auditorium. There is a special tourist transportation to visit the city and see many special places, like the iconic Coyoaacn, San Angel, Xochimilco, Reforma, Chapultepec, and many others.
