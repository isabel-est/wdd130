    • Rafting in San Luis Potosí occurs primarily on the Quetzal River in the Huasteca Potosina region. It is a popular adventure activity that offers breathtaking scenery. The activity includes safety training, complete equipment such as life jackets and helmets, and rapids ranging from moderate adrenaline levels to sections requiring walking and gathering gear later. Our agency often organizes tours in shifts, and some offer additional options such as waterfall jumping.

    Rafting in Veracruz is primarily practiced in Jalcomulco, a popular destination known for the Cihuatl and Pescados rivers, which offer rapids for all levels. It's an ideal activity for those seeking adventure and adrenaline, with options for beginners and experienced rafters.The activity includes safety training, complete equipment such as life jackets and helmets.

    The rafting trip takes place on the Coatl River. It is not a rapids-filled river but rather a scenic journey through waterfalls and pools, where we enter the lush vegetation of the tropical rainforest. Come and enjoy a fun, adventurous tour and follow in the footsteps of the mystical Lacandon people through the lush jungle.
